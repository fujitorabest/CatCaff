About Us

Learn more about CatCaff, our mission, and our furry friends.

Our Story

CatCaff was founded in 2025 with a dream to combine the love of coffee with the love of cats. Starting with three rescue cats, we created a welcoming space where people can relax, enjoy quality coffee, and spend time with our adorable feline friends.

Over the years, CatCaff has grown into a community hub for cat lovers, offering events, adoption opportunities, and a safe haven for rescued cats. Every visit helps us continue our mission to care for cats and provide them with loving homes.

Our Mission

At CatCaff, our mission is simple: to provide a safe, cozy, and engaging environment for both cats and humans. We focus on:

  • Rescuing and caring for cats in need.
  • Promoting adoption to give cats forever homes.
  • Offering a relaxing and enjoyable café experience.
  • Building a community of cat enthusiasts who support animal welfare.

By visiting CatCaff, you're helping us make a positive impact on the lives of cats and people alike.

Meet Our Cats

Our cats each have unique personalities and stories:

  • Mochi: Playful and curious, always exploring new spaces and bringing joy to visitors.
  • Luna: Calm and affectionate, loves curling up in laps for naps and gentle cuddles.
  • Oliver: Adventurous and friendly, he loves interacting with guests and climbing to high spots.
  • Every cat is a rescue, and we strive to ensure they feel loved, safe, and comfortable at CatCaff.

Our Coffee & Menu

We believe coffee should be as comforting as a cat's purr. That's why we partner with local roasters to bring ethically sourced, fresh beans to our café.

Our menu includes classic favorites and seasonal specials:

  • Coffee: Espresso, Latte, Cappuccino, Americano, Iced Matcha Latte, Yerba Mate
  • Snacks: Croissant, Cheesecake, Sandwiches
  • Special Treats: Cat-shaped cookies, Cat treats, Pumpkin Latte

All food and beverages are made with quality ingredients, and vegan or seasonal options are available.

Sustainability & Community

CatCaff is committed to reducing waste and promoting sustainability:

  • We use biodegradable cups and straws whenever possible.
  • We recycle and compost to reduce our environmental footprint.
  • We host community events and workshops to educate about animal welfare and responsible pet ownership.

Visiting CatCaff means supporting not only rescued cats but also a café that cares about people, animals, and the planet.

Fun Facts About CatCaff

  • We have a rotating “Cat of the Month” spotlight to introduce visitors to new furry friends.
  • All our cats are fully vaccinated and cared for by our experienced staff.
  • We organize monthly adoption events for local rescue cats.
  • Visitors can participate in cat yoga sessions and themed cat events.
