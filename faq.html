Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some answers to common questions from our visitors.

Can I bring my own cat?

No, for the safety of all cats, we only allow our resident cats in the café.

Do I need to book in advance?

Yes, we recommend booking in advance to guarantee your spot, especially during weekends.

Is there a time limit for visits?

Yes, visits are usually limited to 3 hours to ensure everyone gets a chance to spend time with the cats.

Are the cats adoptable?

Yes! Many of our cats are rescues looking for forever homes. Our staff will be happy to guide you through the adoption process.

Do you serve food and drinks?

Absolutely! We offer fresh coffee, tea, and a variety of snacks and desserts. Vegan and seasonal options are available.
